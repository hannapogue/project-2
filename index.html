Museum of the House Cat

America's Largest Museum of Cat Paraphernalia!

Welcome to the Museum of the House Cat, a whimsical and enchanting destination dedicated to celebrating the captivating world of our feline friends. Nestled in the heart of a charming town, our museum offers visitors a paw-some opportunity to explore the rich history, artistry, and cultural significance of cats throughout the ages. From ancient Egyptian hieroglyphics honoring revered cat deities to contemporary cat-inspired artworks, our curated exhibits offer a purr-fect blend of education and entertainment for cat enthusiasts of all ages. Step into a world where cats reign supreme, and embark on a delightful journey through the fascinating tales of these enigmatic creatures.

At the Museum of the House Cat, you'll explore diverse exhibits, including an Egyptian cat burial chamber, famous cat-inspired art, and a fun "Cat-astrophe" showcase of fictional feline characters. For an interactive touch, visit "Kitten Corner" to meet adoptable kittens. We offer educational programs and a gift shop with unique cat-themed items, making it a must-visit destination for all cat enthusiasts.
