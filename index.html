Museum of the House Cat

America's Largest Museum of Cat Paraphernalia!

Welcome to the Museum of the House Cat, a whimsical and enchanting destination dedicated to celebrating the captivating world of our feline friends. Nestled in the heart of a charming town, our museum offers visitors a paw-some opportunity to explore the rich history, artistry, and cultural significance of cats throughout the ages.
